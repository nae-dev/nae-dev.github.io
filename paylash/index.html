What is paylash?

paylash is a bill-splitting app for iPhone, iPad, Mac, and the web. Split a single bill on your own with no account, or share groups and expenses with friends through a premium subscription.

Scan receipts with the built-in OCR. Handle uneven splits where only some people had extra items. Lock events to freeze their state and generate clean settlement plans. Share results as PDF or plain text. View read-only from any browser.

No tracking, no personalised ads, no email spam.

Free, no account needed

  • Quick bill splitter with tip presets (5–30%) and a custom tip option
  • Restricted-participation toggle for when only some people had an extra item
  • Itemized split — assign each receipt line to specific people
  • Receipt OCR — Apple Vision framework, all on-device, nothing uploaded
  • Local-only — works offline, no signup, no signin
  • Contextual ? tips on the trickier features, with deep links into the User Guide

Premium features

  • Shared groups across iPhone, iPad, Mac, and the web — real-time sync
  • Events organise expenses by trip, night, or occasion
  • Multiple split styles per expense — equal, restricted, itemized
  • Lockable events with auto-calculated settlement plans (minimum payments)
  • PDF and text settlement reports
  • Push notifications when groups change
  • No ads anywhere in the app
  • 7-day free trial on monthly subscription
