Bellek — Support

Bellek is a memory card game with emoji and flag symbol sets. Below you'll find answers to common questions.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a game?

Choose a grid size from the picker at the top, then tap any card to begin. The countdown starts on your first tap.

What does the Peek (eye icon) do?

Peek briefly reveals all cards for about 1 second. A short rewarded video ad plays before each Peek. You can configure the number of Peeks per game in Settings.

How do I remove ads?

Go to Settings (gear icon) and tap "Remove Ads" ($0.99). This is a one-time purchase that permanently removes all banner and video ads.

I purchased "Remove Ads" but still see ads.

Go to Settings and tap "Restore Purchases". This will sync your purchase with Apple and remove the ads. Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID you used for the purchase.

How do I change the language?

Go to Settings (gear icon) and tap "Language". Bellek supports English, Turkish, German, Spanish, French, and Italian.

How is my score calculated?

Your score is based on the grid size, number of moves, time taken, and consecutive match streaks. Larger grids and fewer moves give higher scores.

Are my scores saved?

Yes. Your top 5 scores per grid size are saved locally on your device. You can view them by tapping the trophy icon. Scores are not synced across devices.

Does Bellek track me or collect personal data?

No. Bellek does not collect any personal information, does not require an account, and does not track you. Ads are contextual (non-personalized). See our Privacy Policy for details.
