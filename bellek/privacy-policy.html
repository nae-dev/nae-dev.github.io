Bellek — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

Overview

Bellek is a memory card game developed by nae-dev. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data is and is not collected when you use the app.

Data We Do Not Collect

Bellek does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically:

  • No accounts or sign-ins are required.
  • No names, email addresses, or contact information are collected.
  • No location data is accessed.
  • No photos, contacts, or other device data are accessed.
  • No analytics or tracking tools are used.
  • No user activity is tracked across other apps or websites.

Advertising

Bellek displays contextual (non-personalized) ads served by Google AdMob. These ads are not based on your personal data or browsing history. The ad network may use anonymous, non-identifying signals for ad delivery, including:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • Country-level location derived from IP address (no precise location)
  • App context (the ad network knows it is serving an ad inside a game)

Bellek does not request permission to track you (no App Tracking Transparency prompt is shown), and no device advertising identifier (IDFA) is accessed.

You can permanently remove all ads by purchasing the "Remove Ads" option within the app.

In-App Purchases

Bellek offers an optional in-app purchase to remove ads. All transactions are handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not process or have access to any payment information.

Children's Privacy

Bellek does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app does not require any personal data to function.

Data Storage

Game settings and high scores are stored locally on your device only. No data is sent to external servers by the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
