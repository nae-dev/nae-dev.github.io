What is rasgele?

rasgele (Turkish for "random") is a clean utility for generating random numbers, letters, names, dice rolls, and lottery picks. No accounts, no clutter — open it, tap, get a result.

Includes a built-in name list manager (up to 50 names), customizable colors, and full localization in six languages.
