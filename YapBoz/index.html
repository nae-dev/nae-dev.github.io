What is YapBoz?

YapBoz (Turkish for "puzzle") is a modern take on the classic sliding-tile puzzle. Slide pieces toward the empty slot — one at a time, or a whole row at once — to recreate the picture.

Choose from beautifully illustrated puzzles spanning museum masterpieces and playful kid-friendly artwork. Pair any picture with the frame style you like — vintage wood, beveled wood, blue gingham — in three difficulty levels.

No accounts, no tracking, no clutter. Just open it and play.

Features

  • Square (3×3, 4×4, 5×5) and portrait (3×4, 4×5, 5×6) puzzles
  • Multiple frame styles, including a vintage hand-photographed board
  • Single-tile and full-row sliding mechanics
  • Light, dark, and system appearance
  • Six languages — English, Turkish, German, Spanish, Italian, French
  • Universal app for iPhone and iPad
  • No personalized ads, no tracking
